My name is Miroslav Kokalanov and I welcome you to my portfolio website.

I'm freelance web developer and graphic designer with more than 10 years of experience in making websites from scratch to deployment, as well as various print graphics. Started as a hobby, it very quickly became a full time job, which I enjoy up to this day.

As a result I have created 250+ fullpack websites, 3 generations of my own CMS framework, 200+ print designs and I've worked with variety of clients from individuals to large companies and institutions.

What I find quite exciting in building websites, is that since web technologies are continuously evolving, you know that there always will be new teritories to unveal.
